Advertise

While we currently aren’t offering advertising, by filling out the crowd watch information for your establishment and keeping current watches, you are doing a service to your customers. They can determine the best times to enjoy your offerings. Let them know if you are packed or empty so that they can make informed decisions.

In the future, we plan to offer location and status based advertising. So if your establishment is in a slow period, you can offer specials to those customers who are looking for a spot that isn’t too crowded, or if a crowd is forming you can pass updates to customers who are looking for a scene. In this manner both you and the customers win. You can keep a revenue stream during the slow hours with customers who enjoy a quieter environment and you can spread the word about when the crowds are forming for customers looking for a scene.

We are openly seeking partnerships, so please contact us with any questions.
