Welcome. This site is barely 1 month old, and like my baby daughter may puke on itself. It is a public alpha release for Coursera's Startup Engineering course. If you are intrigued by the idea of finding out how crowded your favorite places are, please feel free to help in any of the following ways:

  • Report Bugs - The site is still undergoing rapid development and testing. Please report any issues by shooting us an email at poof@coding4kicks.com
  • Tweet or Like - Support people knowing about crowds! While we are in Alpha we would like to keep the site semi-private, but in a few weeks when we enter Beta, please help spread the word.
  • Sign Up and Enter Data - While we are working on ways to help automate crowd detection, this site depends upon data, so any help is appreciated.
       Enter a spot - input your favorite places to go so people can start watching the crowds.
       Write a review - let others know about the places you like for Food, Study, or Social and what the crowds are like.
       Add a CrowdWatch - alert others to the ebb and flow of crowds.
    If we all work together we can avoid crowds or find them, whichever you prefer.
  • Join The Effort - Reach out to us on github.

Thank you. And thanks to Firebase, Angular, Coursera for help making this 1 month prototype possible. And please don't hesitate to email us with any questions: poof@coding4kicks.com.

Regards,

Scott
