How to find a Yelp ID?

If you go to a business's Yelp page, the id is the part of the url with the business name and location.

So if you search for Philz coffee in Palo Alto and go to the first listing, the URL is:
http://www.yelp.com/biz/philz-coffee-palo-alto-2
and the Yelp business ID is:
philz-coffee-palo-alto-2.

There may be a query term after the ID such as #query:coffe if you had found the listing after search for coffee in Palo Alto. Ignore this hash '#' and anything after it.
